--- title: "3D Point Cloud Registration for TeleWindow" excerpt: "
This is my Undergraduate Thesis for Computer Science, as a part of the project TeleWindow, whose purpose was to enhance video conferencing by real-time streaming 3D point-clouds captured by four Intel RealSense Depth Cameras. In this project, we (In group of two) developed a pipeline for efficiently re-constructing the point-clouds with 3D point-cloud registration. We implemented and experimented with several state-of-the-art feature selection, description, and point-cloud registration methods such as SIFT, FPFH, and Colored-ICP. We delivered a pipeline with the best combination that balances effectiveness and efficiency." collection: projects ---

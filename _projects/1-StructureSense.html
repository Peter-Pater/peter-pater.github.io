--- title: "StructureSense" excerpt: "
This is the first project that I led as the first author. This project identifies the potential of building blocks (e.g., furniture parts, Lego blocks, etc.) in fields such as education and interactive fabrication and attempts to create a generalized approach for tracking the assembling process of building blocks. We used an RFID system called IDSense to sense the motion status of the components, and used DBScan infer which components are formed in a structure. Then we used Markov Chain Monte Carlo to sample from the posterior distribution of the possible structures. Finally, we display the results graphically in a Unity simulation. We are finalizing to submit to USIT 2021." collection: projects --- Details will be available soon...
